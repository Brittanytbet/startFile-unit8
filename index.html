Meet our Speakers

We are thrilled to announce our lineup of speakers for the Design Thinking Conference 2026. This year, we have an incredible roster of industry leaders, innovators, and thought-provoking speakers who will share their insights and experiences on design thinking and its impact on various industries.

Our speakers come from diverse backgrounds, including design, technology, business, and academia. They will cover a wide range of topics, from the latest trends in design thinking to real-world case studies and practical applications. Whether you're a seasoned design thinker or just starting your journey, our speakers will provide valuable insights and inspiration to help you navigate the ever-evolving landscape of design thinking.

Dr. Jane Smith

Dr. Jane Smith is a renowned design thinker and innovation strategist with over 20 years of experience in the field. She has worked with leading companies to drive innovation and create impactful design solutions.

Miss. Sarah Johnson

Miss. Sarah Johnson is a visionary entrepreneur and design thinker who has founded several successful startups. She is known for her innovative approach to problem-solving and her ability to inspire teams to think outside the box.

Mr. Michael Lee

Mr. Michael Lee is a leading expert in user experience design and has worked with top tech companies to create intuitive and user-friendly products. He is passionate about using design thinking to improve the lives of users and drive business success.
